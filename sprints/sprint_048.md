# Sprint 048 — ν(q=10) Extraction: Does ν Continue Toward Mean-Field?

**Status:** In progress

## Motivation
ν(q) has a sharp peak at q=4 (≥2.2, BKT-like), then drops: q=5 (2.0), q=7 (0.5). Does ν(q=10) ≈ 0.5 confirm the large-q → mean-field limit? Existing q=10 data (Sprint 043) used χ=10, which is insufficient for d=10 (25% CV inflation at d=7). This sprint reruns with χ=20.

## Key Questions
1. Does ν(q=10) ≈ 0.5 (mean-field), confirming the trend from q=7?
2. Are MI-CV crossings present at q=10 with χ=20? (χ=10 showed crossings, but may be artifacts.)
3. Does the crossing g_c match the scaling law prediction g_c(10)=0.246?
