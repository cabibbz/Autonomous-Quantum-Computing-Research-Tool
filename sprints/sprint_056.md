# Sprint 056 — c(q) Formula: Analytic Continuation & Predictions for q=7,10

**Status:** In progress

## Motivation
We have validated central charges for q=2,3,4,5 Potts (Sprints 054-055). The exact CFT values for q≤4 are known: c(2)=1/2, c(3)=4/5, c(4)=1. For q>4, the 2D classical Potts transition is first-order so no standard CFT exists — but our 1D quantum Potts remains second-order (Sprint 043). c(q=5) ≈ 1.10 ± 0.10 is POTENTIALLY NOVEL.

**Key question:** What formula describes c(q) for the 1D quantum Potts CFT, especially at q>4?

**Approach:**
1. Fit c(q) to various functional forms using q=2-5 data
2. Compute analytic continuation of Coulomb gas formula
3. Measure c(q=7) and c(q=10) via exact diag + DMRG
4. Test predictions against measurements
