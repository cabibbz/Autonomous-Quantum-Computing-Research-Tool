**Date:** 2026-04-02
**Status:** In progress

## Motivation

Symmetry-resolved entanglement entropy (SREE) decomposes total entropy by Z_q charge sector. Literature search confirms: **no one has measured SREE for q>4 Potts or at the walking/complex-CFT boundary.** This is an unexplored diagnostic.

Key questions:
1. Does equipartition hold for our hybrid model at criticality?
2. Does equipartition break down differently in walking (q=5) vs non-walking (q=2,3)?
3. Can charge-resolved entropy provide a sharper walking discriminator than total entropy?

**Literature:** Equipartition conjecture (Goldstein-Sela): at leading order, S_α = S_total/q for Z_q symmetric chains. Subleading corrections ~log(log L)/log L. Only measured for q=2,3 (free fermions, integrable Potts). No q>4 results exist.
